Robert Wiest: A Hometown Advocate

A Greeley, Colorado Native

When it comes to seeking legal counsel in Greeley, Colorado, you're not just looking for an attorney - you're looking for an advocate. A local who knows the intricate tapestry of our community and the nuances of our courts. Robert Wiest is that advocate.

Meet Robert Wiest

Robert Wiest grew up in Greeley, Colorado and went to Greeley West High School. After high school, he went to Colorado State University and got a degree in Political Science. But, he always wanted to be a lawyer. So, he went to Gonzaga University School of Law in Spokane, Washington to study law.

Even though he studied in different places, Robert always knew he wanted to come back to Greeley to be a lawyer. He's been a lawyer since October 1996. For the past 14 years, he's mostly worked on family law - like divorce, child support, and custody.

He doesn't just work on family law, though. He also works on child protection cases, probate matters, and even some criminal law cases. Robert doesn't only work in Greeley. He helps people in Adams County, Morgan County, and Larimer County too.

Why Choose Robert Wiest

For Robert, this isn't just a profession; it's a vocation. It's about connecting with people, understanding their unique narratives, and weaving legal solutions that resonate with their personal stories. If you find yourself facing a legal dilemma, Robert would be honored to help. As a Greeley native, he doesn't just serve the community - he's part of it.
